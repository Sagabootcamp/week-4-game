Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crytals you will add a specifiv amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total goues above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
